# RogueZero At A Glance

## What Is RogueZero

RogueZero is a non-custodial, Solana network automation platform. Users access the platform with a License (validated through KeyAuth) and an Approved Solana Wallet (their owner wallet). They fund an on-chain trading session, and the platform creates a dedicated session wallet to trade on their behalf. The user's funds never leave the chain — every trade is a real, verifiable Solana transaction. The user can stop their session and withdraw at any time.

The platform automates spot token trading through Jupiter — buying and selling SPL tokens based on market signals, technical analysis, and risk controls. It is long-only (no shorts, no leverage, no perpetuals). The goal is to generate consistent profit by making intelligent entry and exit decisions across the Solana token ecosystem.

RogueZero charges a 0.33% performance fee on net session profit. If the bot doesn't make money, we don't make money.
